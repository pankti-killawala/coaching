Why coaching works?
When you face a problem, it is natural for you to seek advice from others (friends, family, colleagues etc). Their advice is based on their personal experiences, stories and cultures; and may not resonate with you completely.

On the other hand, if you are encouraged to find your own answers instead of being told what to do, your brain starts building neural pathways that help you learn from your own experiences and get clarity on the solutions that are appropriate for you. Once you find the solutions that are right for you, you will be excited and motivated to take action.

Coaching helps you find these solutions; making it more powerful than any advice you will ever be given because they are true to who you are. A coach helps you expand on your thinking - he/she is a passenger on your journey, you are the driver and lead the way!
